About the company

From our quarries to your landmark projects

SHRAYFY Marble & Granite is a specialist in natural stone — extracting, processing and supplying marble, granite and our signature shell stone from quarries we own and operate.

Built on long experience in the sector, we control the journey of every slab from the moment it leaves the earth: quarried, cut, finished and delivered. Our focus never changes — high quality, on-time delivery, and meeting each client's exact requirements through our own production and finishing facilities.

  • Uncompromising quality
  • On-time delivery, every order
  • Built around your requirements
Our signature: natural Shell Stone — rich in shells and fossils, with a surface that reads quiet from afar and intricate up close.

Our process

The stone's journey

From the earth's embrace to your project — every block follows a guided path of transformation.

01

Quarried

Blocks are extracted from our own quarries and transported to the plant.

02

Cut into slabs

Blocks are cut into parallel slabs, revealing the beauty within each stone.

03

Treated & reinforced

Resin fills natural pores and cracks, strengthening every slab.

04

Finished & cut to size

Polished or honed, then cut to the exact dimensions your project needs.
